Dates

Fri 24 to Tue 28 Jul 2026. 4 nights — 1 in Inverness, 3 at Staffin (Solas Alainn).

✈️

Route

easyJet London Luton → Inverness, then self-drive the A82/A87 via Loch Ness and the Skye Bridge.

🏔️

Why Skye

The Cuillin, Old Man of Storr, the Quiraing, Fairy Pools, Neist Point sunsets and the wild Atlantic coast.

The stay — Solas Alainn, Staffin

🛏️

Booked

3-bed / 3-bath cottage, sleeps 6 (all king beds, one ground-floor + bathroom). Booking #42401 via Islands & Highlands Cottages. Check-in Sat 25 Jul after 17:00, out Tue 28 by 10:00. Linen, towels, electricity & final clean included.

🏞️

Right under the Quiraing

Detached cottage overlooking Staffin Bay in the far north of Trotternish. Walk to Mackenzie's store & fuel; An Corran (dinosaur) beach and Mealt Falls 5 min; Old Man of Storr 15 min. Side parking, wildflower garden, fast WiFi. Directions land 14 days out.

Planning watchouts

🅿️

Parking fills early

Storr, Quiraing and Fairy Pools fill by mid-morning. Arrive before 09:00; carry the RingGo app and ~£40 in coins.

🛣️

Single-track roads

Trotternish and Glendale are single-track — use the passing places and expect 30–40 mph averages.

⛽

Fuel is sparse

Fill up at Portree or Broadford — don't push past half a tank heading to Neist Point or the Quiraing.

🌦️

Weather flex

Keep the Trotternish and West Skye days swappable until 48h out — the ⛅ swap button on the Itinerary tab flips them in one tap.

🧭

The base is far north

Staffin puts Trotternish on the doorstep — Storr, Kilt Rock, Quiraing and Fairy Glen are all 5–20 min, so give that the clearest day. The trade-off: West Skye (Fairy Pools, Neist) is ~1h15–1h45 each way, so that day starts early and ends late after the sunset.

Good to know

🚗

Car hire & driving

Car hire is booked by a friend, who holds the confirmation. Before departure, share the rental company, lead-driver name, reference, pickup instructions and roadside number. Drive on the left; no tolls — the Skye Bridge is free.

🦟

Midges & ticks

Full-body mesh suits are not needed. Pack a head net, effective midge repellent and lightweight long sleeves/trousers for calm, damp periods. Add a tick-removal tool and check skin after grass, woodland or moorland walks.

💷

Money & data

GBP, contactless everywhere. Carry ~£40 in coins as a parking backup (most use RingGo or card). A UK SIM works fine — no roaming needed; signal drops in parts of Trotternish and Glendale, so download offline Google Maps before you go.

🆘

Emergency & medical

Call 999 or 112 in an emergency. Keep personal medication and insurance details on your own phone. Carry a small first-aid and blister kit; Portree is the main stop for pharmacy and supplies.
