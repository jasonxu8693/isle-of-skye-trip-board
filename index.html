Planning watchouts

🅿️

Parking fills early

Storr, Quiraing and Fairy Pools fill by mid-morning. Arrive before 09:00; carry the RingGo app and ~£40 in coins.

🛣️

Single-track roads

Trotternish and Glendale are single-track — use the passing places and expect 30–40 mph averages.

⛽

Fuel is sparse

Fill up at Portree or Broadford — don't push past half a tank heading to Neist Point or the Quiraing.

🌦️

Weather flex

Keep the Trotternish and West Skye days swappable until 48h out — give Trotternish the clearest forecast.

🧭

The base is far north

Staffin puts Trotternish on the doorstep — Storr, Kilt Rock, Quiraing and Fairy Glen are all 5–20 min, so give that the clearest day. The trade-off: West Skye (Fairy Pools, Neist) is ~1h15–1h45 each way, so that day starts early and ends late after the sunset.

Good to know

🚗

Car hire & driving

Enterprise / Hertz at Inverness airport, mid-size with boot space, ~£200–300 for the 4 days plus ~£40 petrol. Drive on the left, 60 mph single carriageway, 30 in villages. No tolls — the Skye Bridge is free.

🦟

Midges & kit

July–August is midge season — pack Smidge or Avon Skin So Soft and a head net for the Quiraing. Bring waterproof jacket + trousers, hiking boots and layers; weather turns fast.

💷

Money & data

GBP, contactless everywhere. Carry ~£40 in coins as a parking backup (most use RingGo or card). A UK SIM works fine — no roaming needed; signal drops in parts of Trotternish and Glendale, so download offline Google Maps before you go.

🆘

Emergency & insurance

Emergency 999 or 112. Jason on HSBC Premier travel insurance; emergency contact Alice Zhu. Nearest fuel + pharmacy + Co-op are in Portree — don't leave it to the rural ends.
